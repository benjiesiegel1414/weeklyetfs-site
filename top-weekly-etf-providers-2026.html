Top Weekly ETF Fund Providers List 2026

Here are some of the Top Fund providers!


If you are searching for weekly pay ETFs these are some of the strongest fund providers that show up again and again when people look for weekly dividend ETFs. These issuers have built a reputation for consistency high yields and reliable weekly income.

Roundhill Investments

Roundhill is easily one of the biggest names in the weekly ETF space. They run the WeeklyPay series which is designed to pay investors every single week. They offer weekly ETFs tied to major names like AAPL AMD AMZN NVDA and PLTR along with weekly commodity style funds including a weekly gold themed ETF. Roundhill has become a go to choice for investors who want steady predictable income they can rely on.

YieldMax ETFs

YieldMax has made a huge push into the weekly income world. They publish weekly distribution schedules for their option income ETFs and many of their funds now pay every week. YieldMax covers a wide range of underlying assets which means you can choose a weekly ETF that matches your risk style or income goals.

Defiance ETFs

Defiance also shows up on WeeklyPayers as a provider offering weekly dividend ETFs. They focus on leveraged income and enhanced income strategies that appeal to investors who want a little more power behind their weekly payouts.

GraniteShares

GraniteShares is another provider listed on WeeklyPayers. They offer YieldBOOST style ETFs which are known for strong income potential and appear frequently in weekly income ETF lists.

How to Pick a Weekly ETF Provider

If you want simple weekly payouts tied to large cap stocks or commodities Roundhill is an easy starting point. If you want bigger yields and do not mind more aggressive option driven strategies YieldMax gives you a lot of choices. And if you want leveraged or alternative weekly income Defiance and GraniteShares offer weekly paying ETFs that can fit those needs.
